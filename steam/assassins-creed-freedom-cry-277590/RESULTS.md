# Final conclusions

- GE-Proton11-1 launches the verified `ACFC.exe` through DXVK D3D11.
- Very High environment and shadows, maximum textures/reflections, SMAA, HBAO+
  High, High god rays, volumetric fog, and disabled motion blur persist after a
  clean in-game exit.
- The profile does not force a resolution, install a controller layout, add a
  wrapper, or impose an external FPS limit.
- Ubisoft Connect can remain in the tray after the game exits and hold the Steam
  session open; its own Quit action is preferred over killing `upc.exe`.
